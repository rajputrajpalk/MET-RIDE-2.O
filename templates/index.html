About MET RIDE

MET BKC's official carpooling initiative designed to connect students efficiently. It's a real-time carpooling platform that is safe, smart, and student-first.

MongoDB Powered Socket.IO Real-time Flask Backend

Why use MET RIDE?

Built exclusively for the MET BKC community with safety and convenience in mind.

🛡️

SOS Safety Alert

One-tap emergency button that instantly broadcasts your live GPS coordinates to campus admins.

💬

Live Ride Chat

Coordinate pickups effortlessly with real-time group chat rooms powered by Socket.IO.

📍

Geo-Smart Matching

Advanced geospatial indexing finds the closest matching rides and fastest routes to campus.

🪪

Student Verification

A closed ecosystem requiring college ID card upload and institution validation to ensure safety.

🔔

Instant Notifications

Receive push alerts for ride approvals, cancellations, and driver arrivals natively in-browser.

🌙

Midnight Drive UI

A premium dark mode aesthetic (with light mode toggle) to ease eye strain during early commutes.
